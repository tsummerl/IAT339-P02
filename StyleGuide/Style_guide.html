Tale & Tea is a monthly book and tea subscription service. Ever month, subscribers will receive a specially curated box. Each box contains a book, a hot beverage sample (tea, coffee or hot chocolate) and additional extras (such as a book mark or stationary). Boxes are curated by users taking an online quiz and rating past items in boxes

Users can choose to purchase boxes month to month or subscribe yearly for an additional discount.
