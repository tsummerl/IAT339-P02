How it Works

Take our quiz to create a Tale & Tea profile. From there, our curators will create a box specially for you every month, including a new book and some tasty hot beverages to try out!

From your answers, we create a profile tailored just for you. This includes information about your answers like which genres of books, authors, or themes you already enjoy, what kinds of teas and hot beverages you already drink, or how adventurous you are. So what are you waiting for? Get started today!

What to Expect

Other than feeling like it's Christmas morning every month, you can expect nothing but the best reads and the highest quality tea and coffee.

Every box will be different, depending on the month abd your quiz profile. Most boxes will include one book and 2-3 hot beverage packets. Plus we trhow in additional stuff including (but not limited to) stationary, pens, pencils, bookmarks and mugs!

How do you curate boxes?

Our team of book lovers and tea experts are in touch with what is up-and-coming, what is popular, and what a lot of people might not know about. They bring together your quiz profile and their research to create the perfect combination of products that suit your tastes.

Although we keep the exact science behind our product selections a secret, here's what we can tell you about our curators:

  1. They are experts. No one eats up a book or tastes a cup of tea like our people do.
  2. They receive T&T boxes every month, too.
  3. They come from all across the globe, bringing us the best of the best.

If you're interested in becoming a Tale & Tea curator, send us your CV to curators@taleandtea.com.
