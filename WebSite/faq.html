FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our section dedicated to all the Frequently Asked Questions! If your question can't be found here, shoot us an e-mail.

Where do you ship?

We ship everywhere from Kansas to Kathmandu. However, shipping rates may vary.

What does my box include?

Each box may be slightly different depending on the month, or your quiz profile. Most boxes will include one book and 2-3 hot beverage packets.

What if I don't like something in my box?

We stand by the products that we send you, and value your quiz profile. If you don't like something we sent you, just follow these steps:

  • Go to your Account View.
  • Click on Current Box.
  • Find the product you'd like to return.
  • Click the "Return" button next to it.
  • Fill out the return request form.
  • Check your e-mail for an e-mail confirmation from us.
Can I retake my quiz?

Absolutely. Just go to your Account View and click "Take the Quiz". You will have to confirm that you want to override your old answers with your new ones.

How do you curate boxes?

From your answers, we create a quiz profile for you. This includes information about your answers like which genres of books, authors, or themes you already enjoy, what kinds of teas you already drink, or how adventurous you are.

Our team of book lovers and tea experts are in touch with what is up-and-coming, what is popular, and what a lot of people might not know about. They bring together your quiz profile and their research to create the perfect combination of products that suit your tastes.

Although we keep the exact science behind our product selections a secret, here's what we can tell you about our curators:

  1. They are experts. No one eats up a book or tastes a cup of tea like our people do.
  2. They receive T&T boxes every month, too.
  3. They come from all across the globe, bringing us the best of the best.

If you're interested in becoming a Tale & Tea curator, send us your CV to curators@taleandtea.com.

Are there any premium boxes?

We're working on a premium box option for both monthly and yearly subscriptions.

They might include:

  • A second book.
  • Twice as much tea/coffee.
  • A CD.
  • A mug.

If you have any ideas about how to make a premium box awesome, let us know.
