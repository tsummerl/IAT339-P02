How it Works

Take our quiz to create a Tale & Tea profile. From there, our curators will create a box special to you every month, including a new book and some tasty hot beverages to try!

From your answers, we create a quiz profile for you. This includes information about your answers like which genres of books, authors, or themes you already enjoy, what kinds of teas you already drink, or how adventurous you are.

What to Expect

Other than feeling like it's Christmas morning every month, you can expect nothing but the best reads and the highest quality tea and coffee.

Every box will be different, depending on the month, or your quiz profile. Most boxes will include one book and 2-3 hot beverage packets.
